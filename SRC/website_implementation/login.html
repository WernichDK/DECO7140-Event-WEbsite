About this website

Welcome to this music website. We are passionate about music and sharing the freshest, funkiest and grooviest tunes. Register an account today to start liking music, making playlists and interacting with other users.

We strive to foster a sense of inclusivity, we want all of our users to feel safe and included. We encourage everyone to share their taste in music and to interact with others in a kind and respectful manner.

We are currently still in the process of constructing this website so you might notice that some features are not yet fully functional, worry not, we are working hard to guarantee that our music website offers the best music listening experience.

We are working hard to develop the most advanced and accurate music-recommendation system to provide you with new music that you've never heard before and will absolutely love.

While you wait patiently we recommend that you use Soundcloud and/or Spotify in the meantime, even though they don't look as nice :3 lol.
